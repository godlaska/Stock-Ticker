What Are We?

The AFFC Stocks web-client was meticulously crafted as the final project of CSE383 Section A. This dynamic platform is the collaborative work of Keigen Godlaski and Ethan Varner, seamlessly integrating a myriad of concepts learned throughout the semester. Leveraging HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, Ajax, and PHP, we embarked on the mission of creating a multifunctional web application. Our project encompasses essential components like a captivating landing page, informative 'About Us' sections, a comprehensive stock display page, and an insightful stock history page, all intricately intertwined with a robust account system. Behind the scenes, our database operates on phpLite, while our hosting infrastructure relies on OpenStack for seamless server hosting. A pivotal focus of our endeavor was not just functionality but also aesthetics and usability. We aimed for a user-centric design, ensuring that navigating through AFFC Stocks is not only efficient but also visually pleasing. We invite you to explore the culmination of our efforts, and we trust that AFFC Stocks will empower you to seize control of your investments with confidence.
